About Us

What is a Dermatologist?

A Dermatologist is a specialist medical practitioner who is able to diagnose, treat and prevent skin conditions, disease and cancers. They also treat hair and nail disorders. Dermatologists have obtained a medical degree, done at least 2 years of residency and then undertaken 4 years of further training through the Australian College of Dermatologists to obtain specialist qualifications.

Who is Malvern Dermatology?

The Malvern Dermatology and Medical Laser Clinic has been providing specialised dermatology services for over 30 years. Since then the clinic has evolved, and we have expanded our services to include more surgery, laser and cosmetic treatments. We are a family oriented clinic and strive to meet the needs of young children, adolescents, adults and the elderly.
