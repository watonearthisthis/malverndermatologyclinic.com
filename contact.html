Where is Malvern Dermatology?

We are located at 325 Wattletree Road, Malvern East, between Burke and Tooronga Roads. Our clinic is accessible by public transport on the Route 5 Tram along Wattletree Road.

Making an Appointment

ph. 03 9508 9055

Fax 03 9509 3105

Patients are required to call for an appointment. We try to keep our waiting times as short as possible. If your GP feels that the waiting time for appointment that we have given you is too long, and your condition more concerning, we encourage them to contact us for an urgent appointment.

Malvern Dermatology and Medical Laser Clinic is a specialist dermatology clinic. In order to receive your full Medicare rebate entitlement anyone booking an appointment with our dermatologists should have a current referral from a General Practitioner or Medical Specialist. Patients whose referrals are more than one year old, or who do not have a referral, will incur a higher out of pocket expense.

Our Dermatologists have allocated particular days for general consultations, surgery, laser and cosmetic procedures. When you ring for an appointment be sure to advise our reception staff what type of appointment you are after.

What you can expect from your first appointment

During your consultation, your Dermatologist will ask you about the skin concern you have been referred for. If you have more than one concern, or your condition is complicated, we suggest that you advise the reception staff when making your appointment.

Your Dermatologist will discuss your diagnosis and determine the most suitable treatment. If a course of treatment is required, your Dermatologist will discuss the costs involved.
